Our Story

Built by the Community,
for the Community

IMMPC was founded in 2004 with a clear mission: to uplift the lives of ordinary Iligan families through cooperative economics, financial literacy, and collective action. Registered with the Cooperative Development Authority (CDA), we proudly serve thousands of member-families across Lanao del Norte.

Inspired by the natural wealth of Iligan — its roaring waterfalls, lush mountains, and resilient people — we believe in sustainable growth, mutual trust, and shared prosperity for every member.
